Annie's Census Song
(Come Fill Out Our Census)
by Nicki Galland
Sing to the tune of John Denver's Annie's Song 

Come fill out our census
Like a good little voter
Do you live in Wisconsin?
Were you born on a farm?
Are you white or Hispanic?
Or perhaps Ashkanazim?
You told us in '90-
But tell us again.

Come fill out our census
Like that family in Utah
Like those spinsters in Texas
Like your cousin in jail
Did you serve in the army?
Is your income 6 figures?
Do you like macaroni?
Come, tell us again.

Come fill out our census
It will take just a moment.
Do you live with your parents?
Do they think you're a bum?
We won't ask who you sleep with,
But we'll ask how you worship.
Your government's listening -
So tell us again.

Dancing in D.C.: A Selective List

Be warned: I'm partial to swing, lindy, and ballroom, so that's what you'll find the most of here!
  • Ballroom at Maryland (BAM)

    Very active UMD ballroom dance club, with classes in ballroom dance, swing, and lindy, weekly outings, and several big ballroom dances each semester.

  • DC DanceNet

    Info on all styles of dancing in the metro area (swing, lindy, salsa, tango, ballroom, etc.)

  • Glen Echo Park

    A great place for relaxation, recreation, and events, including the area's biggest swing dances, not to mention cajun and zydeco dancing, contra dancing, waltzes, etc. See their weekly calendar of events.

  • Jitterbuzz

    Web page for the serious lindyhoppers in the D.C. area, with weekly events, etc.

  • Washington Post Entertainment Guide

    Good info on nightclubs, dancing, music, theater, and metro area events in general. Used to be easier to find dancing in particular, but unfortunately the navigation is no longer as easy.

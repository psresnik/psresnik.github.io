UMD NLP Lab Computing Facilties

A brief rundown on current NLP laboratory hardware at UMD. This is not CS or UMIACS, but just our NLP labs alone --- CLIP and LAMP. For more on NLP resources (not exhaustive, and organized mostly for lab-internal use) see the Lab pages (especially the Resources link) at http://sparky.umiacs.umd.edu:8000/clip/clip.wiki. 
CLIP 
  - 6 SunBlade 1000s ("dessert" computing cluster)
  - access to 50 node Linux cluster
  - .5 TB RAID
  - approximately 25 workstations including PCs, SunRays, SunUltras, and
    SunBlades (1-2 machines per researcher)
  - full time tech staff
        
  Future plans
    - additional 2-4 SunBlade 1000s
    - upgrades to current SunBlades
    - additional 1 - 2TB devoted to project space

  - approximately 20 Sun Workstations
